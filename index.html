About

The Australia-Vietnam Conference on Smart and Sustainable Agriculture (AVCSSA 2026) is a one-day symposium bringing together researchers, academics, industry representatives, and practitioners from Australia and Vietnam.

The conference provides a forum to share research findings, discuss emerging ideas, and strengthen Australia–Vietnam collaboration across all areas of smart and sustainable agriculture.
