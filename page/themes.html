AVCSSA 2026

Conference Themes

AVCSSA 2026 invites submissions across five interconnected themes that reflect the breadth of smart and sustainable agriculture. Select a theme below to explore its scope and topics of interest.

🌱

Sustainable and Resilient Agricultural Systems

This theme focuses on developing farming practices that can maintain productivity, adapt to environmental stresses, and reduce negative impacts on ecosystems and natural resources.

Contributions are welcome from researchers and practitioners working on agroecological innovation, soil-plant-microbe systems, biodiversity conservation, and climate-adaptive farming at any scale.

Submit to This Theme

Topics of Interest

Biodiversity and Ecosystem Services in Agricultural Landscapes
Food System Resilience and Sustainable Supply Chains
Climate Change Mitigation in Agriculture
Sustainable Plant Disease and Pest Management
Soil Health Restoration and Regenerative Agriculture
Microbiome and Soil–Plant–Microbe Interactions
Agroecological Approaches to Farming System Resilience
Water Use Efficiency and Irrigation Innovation
🤖

Smart and Precision Agriculture

This theme focuses on digital technologies, intelligent systems, automation, and data-driven approaches for next-generation agriculture, with emphasis on sensing, monitoring, control, AI-enabled platforms, robotics, and precision farming technologies.

We welcome work spanning embedded systems and edge computing to large-scale farm management platforms, from autonomous machinery to real-time crop monitoring.

Submit to This Theme

Topics of Interest

Precision farming and site-specific management technologies
IoT, wireless sensor networks, and edge computing for agriculture
AI, machine learning, and computer vision applications in farming
UAVs, drones, and remote sensing technologies
Digital twins, simulation, and predictive analytics for agriculture
Intelligent irrigation and fertigation control systems
Real-time monitoring platforms for crops, soil, livestock, and environment
Agricultural data integration, analytics, and decision-support systems
Embedded systems and cyber-physical systems in agriculture
Automation technologies for harvesting, spraying, and farm operations
🧬

Advanced Breeding, Genomics, and Multi-Omics

This theme focuses on applying modern breeding approaches, genomic technologies, and multi-omics tools to accelerate genetic improvement, enhance productivity, and support climate-resilient and sustainable agriculture.

Contributions spanning crops, livestock, and aquaculture are encouraged, including computational and bioinformatics approaches alongside experimental work.

Submit to This Theme

Topics of Interest

Genomic Selection and Precision Breeding in Crops, Livestock, and Aquaculture
Genome Assembly, Pangenomics, and Population Genomics
Gene Editing and Functional Genomics for Trait Improvement
Multi-Omics Integration: Genomics, Transcriptomics, Proteomics, Metabolomics, Epigenomics, and Microbiomics
Breeding for Climate Resilience, Disease Resistance, Productivity, and Quality Traits
Conservation and Utilisation of Genetic Resources and Biodiversity
High-Throughput Phenotyping and Digital Tools for Breeding
Bioinformatics, AI, and Data-Driven Approaches in Genomic Research and Breeding
🏛️

Policy, Governance, and Social Inclusion in Agriculture

This theme focuses on the role of policy, governance, and inclusive development in enabling sustainable, resilient, and equitable agricultural transformation.

It explores how institutions, regulatory frameworks, participatory approaches, and innovation systems can strengthen food security, climate resilience, rural livelihoods, and fair access to resources, technology, markets, and decision-making across agri-food systems.

Submit to This Theme

Topics of Interest

Agricultural policy reform, food security strategies, rural transformation, and agricultural financing
Governance systems, institutional effectiveness, decentralisation, and participatory decision-making in agriculture and rural development
Land, water, and natural resource governance, including tenure security and biodiversity conservation
Cooperatives, farmer organisations, multi-stakeholder partnerships, and community-based agricultural governance
Gender equality, youth participation, Indigenous knowledge, and social inclusion in agricultural development
Equity in access to land, finance, technology, extension services, markets, and digital infrastructure
Social protection, labour dynamics, migration, and support for vulnerable rural communities
Climate-smart agriculture, agroecology, disaster risk management, and food systems resilience
Digital governance, agricultural innovation systems, ICT access, data governance, and smart farming policy
Agricultural productivity, value chains, trade, and inclusive rural economic development
Ethical, legal, and emerging issues in agriculture, including governance during crises, climate shocks, and pandemics
Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), global food systems governance, and international agricultural policy cooperation
📈

Productivity, Agri-Economics, and Value Chains

This theme focuses on the economic, productivity, and value chain dimensions of agricultural transformation in the context of sustainability, climate change, digitalisation, and global market integration.

The theme explores how innovation, technology adoption, policy, finance, and market systems can improve agricultural productivity, resilience, competitiveness, and inclusive growth across agri-food systems.

Submit to This Theme

Topics of Interest

Agricultural productivity, efficiency, and performance analysis
Labour productivity, workforce transformation, and mechanisation in agriculture
Economics of digital agriculture, AI, and technology adoption
Agricultural finance, insurance, investment, and risk management
Agricultural trade, export competitiveness, and global market integration
Consumer behaviour, food standards, traceability, and certification systems
Agri-food value chains, logistics, and supply chain resilience
Agribusiness development, entrepreneurship, and innovation ecosystems
Food security, food systems transformation, and nutrition economics
Climate risk, adaptation, and resilience economics in agri-food systems
Green growth, ESG, circular economy, and low-emission agricultural value chains
Economic modelling, policy analysis, and data-driven decision-making in agriculture and food systems

Ready to contribute?

Submit your abstract by 1 June 2026. Oral and poster presentations welcome across all five themes.
