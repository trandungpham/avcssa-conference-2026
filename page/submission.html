Abstract Submission

Deadline: 1 June 2026

Submission Guidelines

Abstract Format

  • Maximum 300 words
  • Written in English
  • Include title, authors, affiliation, and keywords
  • Indicate preferred presentation type: Oral or Poster

Themes

  • Sustainable and Resilient Agricultural Systems
  • Advanced Breeding, Genomics, and Multi-Omics
  • Smart and Precision Agriculture
  • Agricultural Productivity, Value Chains, and Economics
  • Policy, Governance, and Social Inclusion in Agriculture

Topics of Interest

  • Biodiversity and Ecosystem Services in Agricultural Landscapes
  • Food System Resilience and Sustainable Supply Chains
  • Climate Change Mitigation in Agriculture
  • Sustainable Plant Disease and Pest Management
  • Soil Health Restoration and Regenerative Agriculture
  • Microbiome and Soil–Plant–Microbe Interactions
  • Agroecological Approaches to Farming System Resilience
  • Water Use Efficiency and Irrigation Innovation

Submission Process

  1. Submit via the online submission portal before 1 June 2026
  2. A confirmation email will be sent automatically on receipt
  3. Outcomes notified by 15 June 2026
